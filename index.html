Most expensive cities in the world to live in

The most important cities in the world are often also the most expensive to live in.
Below, we will discuss the top five most expensive cities to live in.

Why live in a city?

Living in a city often has its advantages. For instance, there is good public transport which means you no longer need a car. Additionally, a lot of different cultures come together, shops and various other things are easy to reach and there are plenty of job opportunities (Lettings, z.d.).

  • 2. Singapore
  • 3. Tel-Aviv
  • 4. Los Angeles
